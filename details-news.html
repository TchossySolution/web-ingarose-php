By Berta Conde Issa

Towards sustainable economy innovation and interconnectivity for social development

Berta Issa , Managing Director and Founder of INGA ROSE, participates in the main initial investment event that fills the funding gap between the Francophone, Anglophone, Lusophone and Arabophone markets.

The Tropics Business Summit (TBS) is one of the main investment events that connects start-ups, investors, more than 500 participants and 250 speakers and this year has as its main theme "Towards a Sustainable Economy: Innovation and Interconnectivity for Social Development ''

Angola, which will participate for the first time in this event, will be attended by professionals, who will address the following topics in 10-minute panels on Thursday 15 October, from 13:

Berta Rodrigues Issa: “Education and Gender inequality: when cultural tradition is a factor of exclusion”; Eunice Carvalho (Directora Geral da Unitel para os Assuntos Corporativos): "Empowering African youth through telecommunications"; Emanuel Zeferino (EZ Engineering Group - Angola): “Strategic Decision, a key factor for business and economic sustainability”; Sara Almeida (Directora de Operações Global Finance and Investment Company): "Digitalization in the insurance industry"

The event will take place from 14 to 17 October 2020, from the following channels: facebook and YouTube.
