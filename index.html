About

  Us
TRANSFORMING YOUR VISION TO REALITY

Inga Rose is an Angolan based and owned consulting and technical services company focused on Strategy and Management. We help clients identify, develop and implement projects and structure operations in challenging environments. Our boldness shapes the future and drives positive change. Inga Rose adds real value to business performance in operational, procurement and supply chain, financial, commercial, human capital and administrative areas.
