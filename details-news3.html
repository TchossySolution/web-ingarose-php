MASFAMU in partnership with INGA ROSE, have developed the More Inclusion Project

The More Inclusion project aims to evaluate the implementation of these policies in macro public and private structures through a collection of statistical data.

These data will be useful to describe the roles that women, men young people and people with disabilities play in society and the economy in order to provide a basis, for promoting strategies and policy-making. That can contribute to the integration of the groups referenced above while also enabling, sound monitoring and evaluation of the effectiveness of the policies under reference to be ensured.

The first phase of the project, underway since 2019. Compiled in a period of 9 months statistical data from the main macrostructures of the country and is currently the team evaluating the implementation of policies promoting inclusion in public and private institutions.

On this path, MASFAMU has held a Round Table, conducted by INGA ROSE. The topics will be given in plenary, where the speakers stimulated the sharing of ideas among the participants

The event took place at the Ministry of Social Action Family and Women's Promotion, on the following dates:

  • First Round Table, on June 4000 9:00 am to 12:00 pm
  • Second Round Table, june 1800 09:00 to 12:00
  • Third Round Table, june 2500 09:00 to 12:00
  • Fourth Round Table, September 15 from 09:00 to 12:00
